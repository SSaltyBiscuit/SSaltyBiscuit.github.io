HNRS 2020 Project

For my first artifact I chose a the ticket for an opera that I went to wirth my friends it the opera was put on by the Toledo Opera in the Valentine Theater



Why?



Well, one night in November my roomate and I were sitting around playing a game together and he said to me "Why don't we go to the opera." Now he was obviously joking but it got me thinking. Why don't we go to the opera. So, I went to the website and went and purchased three tickets (one for me, one for my roomate, and one for my friend) for the an opera that was showing 3 months from now. It was a bit of an impulse buy but I always was the kind of person to choose to live in the moment.



So, finally the day came to go to the opera and oh boy were we pumped. We dressed in our finest silks, and donned in the our most affluent jewels we began the drive to Toledo. When we get there we are greeted by the person who checks your tickets and she asks is this your first opera. We say yes and her face instantly lights up and she begins to start to list some fun facts and other things of that sort. Well at the end she asks are you guys performing arts or music students, and my roomate instantly responds no we just thought it would be fun to go to the opera. But this gets us thing what if we were music students. So, anytime anyone asks us if we are music students (everyone asks this) we'll just say yes make up our own personal view of what we would be if we were music majors. My friend he's studying the chello, my roomate is majoring in music production, and I specialize in the tuba. Now while this is a pretty bold faced lie nobody is getting hurt (no harm no fowl right). So we chat with a few people in our new personas before eventually sitting down in our assigned seats and getting ready to enjoy the opera. Now we had the best seats in the house all the way at the tippy top of the theater (they were the cheapest seats you could get). The opera began and I have to say perhaps I should of done a little bit more research as it seems the whole thing was in french. Fast forward 2 hours and we are at the intermission. Not to say that my friends and I weren't enjoying the graceful sopranos and the powerful baritones but it was clear that we were in no mood to stay for the rest of the performance.



We, piled into my friends dilapidated 2006 Honda Civic
